About Us

Kulin Shah leads Amay Exim Inc as its CEO and Managing Director. He brings a wealth of knowledge from his 30 years working in the timber industry, where he has built a solid reputation for reliability and top-notch service. The company's headquarters are in Canada, which is where all the administrative and coordination tasks take place. This location is strategic as it allows for smooth operations across different time zones and markets.

The heart of Amay Exim Inc's operation is in Brazil. Brazil is known for its vast forests and is one of the world’s leading sources of timber. By situating the core operations there, Amay Exim Inc can directly access these resources. This direct access helps the company maintain a steady supply of timber and allows them to oversee the production process closely, ensuring that everything runs efficiently. The company’s international presence is strong, with a track record of successfully shipping their timber products to various countries across the globe. These countries include major markets in Asia, such as China, Japan, Vietnam, South Korea, and Singapore, as well as India and regions like the Middle East and South-East Asia. This wide-reaching network showcases Amay Exim Inc's capability to handle complex international demands and logistics.

Throughout its history, Amay Exim Inc has always prioritized maintaining high standards of quality and adopting eco-friendly practices. This approach has been fundamental to the company's success and reputation in the timber industry. By committing to these values, Amay Exim Inc ensures that its products are not only of excellent quality but also sourced and produced responsibly. Currently, the company is excited to offer a supply of Eucalyptus Grandis logs. These logs are a type of hardwood that is in high demand for its durability and versatility. They are carefully harvested from the Brazilian forests, where the company has the advantage of overseeing the entire process
