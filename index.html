About Us

About Us Mr. Kulin Shah is the CEO & Managing Director of Amay Exim Inc, with our corporate office based in Canada. With over 30 years of experience in the timber industry, we have built a legacy of trust and excellence.

While our administrative roots lie in Canada, our core operations are strategically positioned in Brazil. This unique setup allows us to tap into Brazil's rich timber resources while coordinating seamlessly from our Canadian base. Our global reach encompasses successful exports to numerous countries, including China, Japan, Vietnam, South Korea, Singapore, India,, and regions within the Middle East and South-East Asia.

Throughout our illustrious journey, Amay Exim Inc has remained devoted in its commitment to quality and sustainable practices, ensuring unparalleled excellence in every offering. We are pleased to present our distinguished proposal for the supply of Eucalyptus Grandis logs, meticulously harvested and processed in Brazil to meet the highest industry standards.
